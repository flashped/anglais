The Journey

Your Week
in Cuba

Day 1 — 2
LaHavana

Arrive at José Martí Airport, settle in Old Havana, then explore El Capitolio, Plaza de la Catedral, and local markets. End with a Cuban cooking class — ropa vieja on the menu.

Old Havana El Capitolio Plaza de la Catedral Cooking Class
Day 3
Valle deViñales

UNESCO World Heritage Valley surrounded by dramatic mogote mountains. Horseback ride or hike through tobacco fields, then discover how the famous Cuban cigars are made.

Mogote Hike Horseback Ride Tobacco Farm Cuban Cigars
Day 4
Pearl ofthe South

Cienfuegos — where French elegance meets Cuban spirit. Stroll past colonial squares, grand historic buildings, and enjoy the calm of the southern coast.

Cienfuegos French Heritage Colonial Squares Seafront
Day 5
HistoricTrinidad

UNESCO cobblestone streets frozen in time. Browse Plaza Mayor, discover local artisans, then close the day with a salsa class — the heartbeat of Cuban culture.

Plaza Mayor Cobblestone Streets Local Artisans Salsa Class
Day 6 — 7
PlayaAncón

Cuba's finest white sand beach. Snorkel, dive, or simply soak in the Caribbean sun. End the trip with a farewell dinner and live music before flying home from Havana.

Snorkeling Diving White Sand Beach Farewell Dinner
